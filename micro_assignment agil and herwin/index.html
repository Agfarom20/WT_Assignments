About Us!

At Musife, we bring together the best of music and food. Our café offers a cozy atmosphere where you can enjoy delicious meals, handcrafted drinks, and live performances from local artists. Whether you're here for a quick bite or to relax with live music, we promise a memorable experience every time.

Where to find Us?

You can find Musife in the heart of Nungambakam, just a few steps from Melody Park. We're open daily.

Hours:
Monday – Friday: 8 AM – 10 PM
Saturday – Sunday: 9 AM – 11 PM
