World of Musical Instruments

Guitar

The guitar is a string instrument known for its versatility and widespread use in genres such as rock, jazz, and classical music. It typically has six strings and is played with fingers or a pick.

Keyboard

The keyboard is an electronic instrument that simulates the sound of various instruments, including the piano. It is widely used in modern and classical music.

Saxophone

The saxophone is a wind instrument commonly used in jazz, classical, and popular music. Its rich tone and expressive range make it a favorite among musicians.

Violin

The violin is a bowed string instrument, often referred to as the "queen of instruments." It is known for its high-pitched, expressive sound and is central to orchestral music.

Accordion

The accordion is a portable instrument that produces sound by compressing air through reeds. It is popular in folk, classical, and contemporary genres.

Cello

The cello is a bowed string instrument larger than a violin but smaller than a double bass. It produces a deep, rich sound and is a staple in classical music ensembles.
